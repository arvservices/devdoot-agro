The Legacy

A Heritage of Purity Since 1997

Devdoot Trading Co., established in 1997, began as a modest seed and spice trading business in the heart of Gujarat. Over the decades, it evolved into DEVDOOT AGRI FOOD INDUSTRIES LLP — a globally trusted name in premium agricultural exports.

Today, we source directly from certified farms across Gujarat and Rajasthan, process with state-of-the-art Sortex and gravity separator machinery, and export to over 15 countries across the Middle East, South-East Asia, Europe, and Africa.

Every grain that leaves our facility carries our promise: uncompromising purity, strict adherence to international food safety standards, and the authentic essence of Indian soil.

Our Journey

Decades of Excellence

From humble beginnings to global exports, discover the milestones that define Devdoot Agro.

1997

The Foundation

Established as Devdoot Trading Co. in Halvad, Morbi, Gujarat. We began our journey trading premium local spices and seeds within domestic markets, building strong relationships with local farmers and mandi traders.

2005

First Major Expansion

Invested in modern seed cleaning and grading machinery. Expanded operations beyond local markets to pan-India distribution, serving wholesalers in Mumbai, Delhi, and Chennai.

2012

Quality Certifications

Obtained ISO and FSSAI certifications. Installed Sortex color-sorting technology to achieve 99.5% purity levels, setting the stage for international export readiness.

2018

Going Global

Transitioned into DEVDOOT AGRI FOOD INDUSTRIES LLP. Successfully executed first major international export orders to the Middle East and South-East Asia, bringing authentic Indian flavors to the world.

2022

Multi-Continent Reach

Expanded export footprint to Europe, Africa, and the Americas. Established dedicated quality control labs and introduced batch-level traceability for complete supply chain transparency.

2026

A Modern Exporter

Today, we proudly export an extensive catalog of premium agro-products to 15+ countries worldwide, recognized globally for our unwavering commitment to quality, transparency, and on-time delivery.
