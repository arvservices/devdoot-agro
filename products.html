Cumin Seeds

Renowned for its strong, earthy flavor and warm aroma, our premium Cumin Seeds (Jeera) are carefully harvested from the fertile soils of Gujarat and Rajasthan. Each batch is rigorously sorted through Sortex color-sorting machines and gravity separators to meet strict international food safety standards. Rich in essential oils like cuminaldehyde and thymol, they are an essential ingredient in Indian, Middle Eastern, Mexican, and Mediterranean cuisines. Cumin is also valued for its digestive and antioxidant properties.

  • Quality: Premium Export Grade (Machine Cleaned)
  • Moisture: Below 9%
  • Purity: 99% / 99.5% Options
  • Packaging: 25kg / 50kg PP Bags (Custom available)

Coriander Seeds

Our Coriander Seeds (Dhania) are naturally green, highly aromatic, and completely free from artificial colors or preservatives. Sortex machine-cleaned and gravity-separated to ensure zero impurities, they offer an authentic, citrus-like flavor profile suitable for both grinding and whole spice usage. Grown in the Saurashtra and Rajasthan regions, our coriander is rich in linalool — the essential oil responsible for its distinctive aroma — and is widely used in curry powders, marinades, and baking across the globe.

  • Type: Eagle / Scooter / Parrot green
  • Moisture: Below 9%
  • Split/Broken: Less than 5%
  • Packaging: 25kg / 50kg PP Bags

Fennel Seeds

Recognized globally for its sweet, licorice-like flavor and powerful digestive benefits. Our Fennel Seeds (Saunf) are carefully graded by color, size, and essential oil content. Grown primarily in the Unjha and Saurashtra regions of Gujarat — the world's largest fennel-producing belt — our seeds are exported to the Middle East, Europe, and the Americas. They are a staple in both culinary recipes (Italian sausages, Indian mukhwas, herbal teas) and traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

  • Type: Singapore Quality / Europe Quality
  • Purity: 98% / 99% / 99.5%
  • Moisture: Max 10%
  • Packaging: 25kg / 50kg PP Bags
